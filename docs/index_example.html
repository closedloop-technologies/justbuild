Bridging Mental Models

In the world of software development, three distinct mental models shape how we process and create information:

Human Thinking: Graphs

We visualize complex interactions and generate intricate plans, forming interconnected networks of ideas.

AI Language Models: Linear

AI models process and generate information sequentially, outputting step-by-step instructions or continuous text.

Computer Programs: Trees

Software operates on hierarchical structures, seen in abstract syntax trees and data organizations.
